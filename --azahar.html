This beautiful 43 hectares farm, is a paradisiacal jewel in a magical place, surrounded by lush cloud rainforest. A spectacular entrance to the farm is a river, with a warm fall of currents, and a delight for those who enjoy watching the crystal clear waters. It has delicate hills immersed in gardens, a lake and water mirrors. The visit of quetzals are one of the most important attractions of this property.

The property presents perfect conditions for living, for a family hotel, a retirement community, a boutique hotel or an organic farm. You could work cattle ranching, make a dairy, raise horses and many other uses.

The property already has a beautiful 450m2 house with 3 bedrooms and 3 full bathrooms. It also has an office, large kitchen and laundry, wine cellars, guest areas and a beautiful deck with a spectacular view of the mountains. It is surrounded by gardens full of life all around the house that reach a lake surrounded by gardens and vegetation.

It has internal roads throughout the property (in good condition). The whole farm is divided into paddocks with electric wire for the cattle. It has a stable where sheep were kept in the past. It is totally enclosed. It has a house for a worker and his family. It has two stables to keep the cattle. As additional valuables, there are several rivers and springs throughout the property. The entire farm is surrounded by primary cloud forest. ...
